Terraria, but with Technology!

Construct multitile machines with the Science Workbench and explore the wonders of technology!


Current Multitiles:
Salt Extractor - provide Water or Saltwater to produce Salt

Science Workbench - constructs machinery

Reinforced Furnace - burn wooden items to produce Coal

Air Ionizer - currently does nothing

Electrolyzer - currently does nothing

Blast Furnace - powered by coal, increases the gains from smeling ores

Basic Wind Turbine - generates Terra Flux (TF) based on the current wind speed and weather

Basic Battery - stores TF
